Chicken or Pork Adobo

Description

Adobo is a classic Filipino dish made by simmering meat in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, creating a rich, savory-sour sauce. It’s a staple comfort food known for its bold flavor and tender meat.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken or pork (or a mix)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 6 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 2–3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instruction

  1. In a pot, combine meat, soy sauce, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Add vinegar and water (don’t stir yet), then bring to a boil.
  3. Once boiling, lower heat and simmer for 30–45 minutes until tender.
  4. (Optional) Add brown sugar for a slightly sweet taste.
  5. Let the sauce reduce to your preferred thickness. Serve with rice.
