Bulalo (Beef Marrow Soup)

Description

Bulalo is a hearty beef marrow soup made by slowly simmering beef shanks until the meat is fall-off-the-bone tender. Served with vegetables like corn and cabbage, it’s perfect for cold days or special gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef shank with bone marrow
  • 1 onion (quartered)
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 5–6 cups water
  • 1 corn on the cob (cut into 3–4 pieces)
  • 1 bundle pechay or napa cabbage
  • 2 potatoes (quartered)
  • 1 saba banana (optional, sliced)
  • Fish sauce or salt to taste
  • Whole peppercorns

Instruction

  1. In a large pot, boil beef in water with onion and peppercorns. Skim off scum.
  2. Lower heat and simmer for 2–3 hours until beef is tender and marrow is soft.
  3. Add potatoes and corn. Simmer for another 10–15 minutes.
  4. Add pechay or cabbage and cook for 2–3 more minutes.
  5. Season with fish sauce. Serve hot with rice.
