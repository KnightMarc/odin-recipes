Sinigang na Baboy (Pork in Sour Tamarind Soup)

Description

Sinigang is a sour Filipino soup typically made with pork and vegetables, flavored with tamarind or sinigang mix. Its tangy broth is both refreshing and comforting, often enjoyed with a side of rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or ribs
  • 1 onion (quartered)
  • 2 tomatoes (quartered)
  • 1 radish (sliced)
  • 1 radish (sliced)
  • 1 eggplant (sliced)
  • 1 bundle kangkong or water spinach
  • 11 packet sinigang mix (or fresh tamarind if available)
  • 6–7 cups water
  • Long green chili (optional)
  • Fish sauce or salt to taste

Instruction

  1. Boil pork in water with onion and tomatoes. Simmer until meat is tender (around 1–1.5 hours).
  2. Add radish and eggplant, cook for 5–7 minutes.
  3. Pour in sinigang mix and stir. Adjust sourness to taste.
  4. Add kangkong and chili, cook for 1–2 minutes.
  5. Season with fish sauce. Serve hot with rice.
